About Me

Hi! I'm Scotti Argyle and I currently work in Application Support for a title insurance company.


I started working in my company as an Escrow Assistant in 2021. I was able to quickly learn and grow in that position and within four months was assisting the busiest escrow closer in the Wichita area. After working in that position for just over a year, our company made a switch to a brand new software. This new software was rolled out under some extenuating circumstances and as such was not fully developed or ready for use.


We were building a plane as we were flying it.

I had the opprotunity to learn this software and was able to quickly learn, navigate, and teach my peers. This led me to a new role within the company working with our project manager and a small team to lead the roll out. This involved training, developing, testing and helping build materials to guide staff.



This new roll inspired more advanced learning and exploration in the realm of software development. Since beginning this role I have taken it upon myself in my off time to learn front end development. This has helped me build confidence in my communication between the end user and our developers. It has also opened up a new passion for coding.



I have used this new found passion to learn HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Git, Github, and NodeJS. I have had the opprotunity to build some great projects which I hope you will enjoy viewing below.
